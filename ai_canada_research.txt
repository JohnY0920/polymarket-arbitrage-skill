AI Business Benefits and Funding Opportunities in Canada - 2024/2025

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Canada has committed $2.4 billion in Budget 2024 to advance AI adoption, with significant opportunities for businesses in manufacturing, packaging, and technology sectors.

KEY FUNDING PROGRAMS:

1. AI Compute Access Fund
- Up to $5 million per company
- For AI compute resources and infrastructure
- SMEs developing AI solutions

2. Regional Artificial Intelligence Initiative (RAII)
- $33.8 million over 5 years (2024-2029)
- Focus: Prairies region AI adoption
- Sectors: Manufacturing, clean tech, healthcare

3. NRC IRAP AI Assist Program
- $750K to $10.5 million per project
- Up to 100% of eligible costs
- For Canadian SMEs

ONTARIO TAX CREDITS:

1. Ontario Innovation Tax Credit (OITC)
- 8% refundable tax credit
- For R&D expenditures in Ontario

2. SR&ED Program
- 15% refundable (federal) + 3.5-10% (Ontario)
- For AI development and R&D

3. OIDMTC (Digital Media)
- 30-40% of eligible labour costs
- For AI-powered digital solutions

RECENT INVESTMENTS:
- $2.4 billion total AI commitment (Budget 2024)
- $2 billion Sovereign Compute Strategy
- $936.8 million in AI research since 2017

BUSINESS BENEFITS:
- 20-40% cost reduction in manufacturing
- 25-35% quality improvement
- 30-50% faster time-to-market
- 12-24 month payback period
- 25-40% IRR for well-implemented projects

RECOMMENDATIONS:
1. Apply for AI Compute Access Fund
