## Common Use Cases

### Startup/Early Stage
- Type: grants and funding
- Goal: start or buy a business
- Amount: up to $50,000

### Growth/Expansion
- Type: loans and capital investments
- Goal: grow and expand my business
- Amount: $500,000 and above

### R&D/Innovation
- Type: grants and funding, researchers and facilities
- Goal: conduct R&D, innovate, develop a product or service
- Amount: varies

### Export/International

- Goal: sell internationally
- Selling internationally: active or planning

### Hiring/Training
- Type: wage subsidies and interns
- Goal: hire or train employees
- Amount: varies

## Tips for Effective Matching

1. **Be specific when possible** - More filters = better matches
2. **Start broad, then refine** - Can always narrow results
3. **Select multiple options** - Many questions allow multiple selections
4. **Consider all support types** - Don't limit to just funding
5. **Think about timing** - Some programs have application deadlines
6. **Check eligibility carefully** - Each program has specific requirements

---

## Important Notes

- Programs are from federal, provincial, and municipal levels
- Eligibility varies by program (size, sector, location, etc.)
- Many programs require matching funds or co-investment
- Application processes and timelines differ
- Some programs are always open; others have specific intake periods
- Support can be financial (grants, loans) or non-financial (advice, connections)
