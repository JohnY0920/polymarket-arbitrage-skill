- **Type**: Tax credit (refundable)
- **Amount**: 35% refund on first $3M of R&D spending (15% thereafter)
- **Best for**: Any R&D work, including AI algorithm development
- **Eligibility**: Canadian corporations doing R&D in Canada
- **Why it's perfect**: AI consulting work often qualifies as R&D

### 3. **CanExport Innovation**
- **Type**: Grant
- **Amount**: Up to $75,000 per project
- **Best for**: Exporting AI services internationally
- **Eligibility**: Canadian company with <500 employees, new to target market

### 4. **Ontario Innovation Tax Credit (OITC)**
- **Type**: Tax credit
- **Amount**: 8% refundable tax credit on R&D expenditures
- **Best for**: R&D activities in Ontario
- **Eligibility**: Ontario-based corporations doing R&D
- **Why it's perfect**: Stacks with federal SR&ED for maximum benefit

- **Type**: Grant + zero-interest loan
- **Amount**: Up to $15,000 for advisory services + $100,000 loan
